Track-A-Mole is the ultimate tool for monitoring your skin health. Using a sophisticated 3D human model, you can accurately map the location of your moles and track changes over time. 

Key Features:
- 3D Mole Mapping: Visualize your moles on a realistic 3D body model.
- Photo tracking: Capture and store photos of your moles to monitor development.
- Secure storage: All your data is stored locally on your device.
- Easy to use interface designed for skin health awareness.

Disclaimer: Track-A-Mole is for personal tracking and educational purposes only. It is not intended for medical diagnosis or as a substitute for professional medical advice.
